Appearance: Her bright purple hair comes down half way down her back, contrasting with her electric blue armor and gauntlets. She tends to wear a cocky smile in this form, which she can certainly back up with all the muscle hiding under her armor. 

Affiliation: Herself

Personality: She's quite the character, sporting a fair set of quirks. Despite her roll as the World's Former Greatest Demon Empress she's quite kind. Taken a shine to children and helping strangers when they show her kindness. She seems to have taken a liking to your party, even helping you when she sees fit. 

Fighting Style: She prefers hand to hand combat, tending to tush down opponents in order to relentlessly pummel them. When this doesn't work she has quite a few lightning spells at her command. Keeping her at range seems to be a viable strategy, but her considerable speed makes this strategy almost moot. Some sort of stunning or trapping magic would be the best way to go.
